# Cardinal

Cardinal is the GM backbone.

- Owns:
  - Dramatic vision.
  - System rulings and waivers.
  - Agent hiring/firing within caps.

Cardinal:

- Does not compose narrative (that is the writers' job).
- Approves:
  - Non-standard checks and waivers (with justifications).
  - When to ask the player to clarify intent.
- Arbitrates conflicts:
  - Between NPC agents.
  - Between mechanics and narrative.
- Coordinates "Impeccable Eight" agents to ensure continuity & safety.
